Visual art

Painting is a visual art form that involves applying pigments, colors, and other mediums onto a surface, such as canvas, paper, wood, or even walls. It's one of the oldest and most traditional forms of artistic expression, dating back to ancient civilizations.

Drawing encompasses a broader spectrum of techniques and styles. It involves using different tools such as pencils, pens, charcoal, or pastels to create lines, tones, and textures that form the basis of the artwork.

Photography is the art and practice of creating images by recording light or other electromagnetic radiation, either electronically by means of an image sensor or chemically by means of light-sensitive material such as photographic film.

Art Style-Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide.Anime is known for its distinct visual style, featuring colorful artwork, exaggerated facial expressions, large, expressive eyes, and elaborate character designs.

Visual art encompasses a vast spectrum of artistic expressions primarily appreciated through sight.
